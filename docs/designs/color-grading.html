Interaction Model
Puck drag: pointer down anywhere inside the disk → move puck to cursor, clamping to disk radius. Puck cannot leave the disk boundary.

Luma ring drag: pointer down on ring arc or anywhere within the ring band → vertical drag adjusts master value; rotational drag along arc also works. A clockwise rotation increases master brightness.

Double-click puck: resets hue + chroma to center; luma ring unchanged.

Double-click numeric field: resets that single parameter to its default (0.00 for R/G/B, 0.00 for M).

Scroll on field: increments or decrements the field value by step (0.01 for wheel fields, 1 for strip fields).

All changes update preview canvas in real time. Panel close records one undo entry.
Panel Sizing & Spacing
Panel width: 504 px — driven by 4 × 119 px wheel blocks + 3 × 4 px gaps + 2 × 8 px horizontal padding.

Wheel canvas: 96 × 96 CSS px (rendered at 2× for retina). Disk radius 33 px; luma ring inner 36 px, outer 47 px (11 px thick), gap 3 px.

Top / bottom strips: 6 rows total — label 9 px + gap 3 px + slider 8 px + gap 3 px + value 16 px = 39 px strip height + 11 px padding = 50 px.

Font sizes: 9 px (wheel channel labels, strip labels), 10 px (numeric fields), 11 px (title, footer). Consistent with other Verve panels.

Title bar: 26 px, same as all other adj panels. Includes Preview toggle (matches accent-glow when active) and × close.
