Error toast — selection too small
Selection is too small for content-aware fill.
Appears at the bottom of the workspace when selection is smaller than 4 px in any dimension. Also shown if the WASM module is not yet ready. Toast fades out after ~4 s. No changes are made to layers.
Behaviour notes
Single-click operation — no settings dialog. Everything is automatic.
Content-Aware Fill: active layer unchanged, new fill layer inserted above.
Content-Aware Delete: selected pixels erased on active layer; fill inserted above.
Undo: single undo step restores both the erasure and the fill layer atomically.
Source sampling: PatchMatch composites all visible layers for context — hidden layers are ignored.
Fill layer: pixels outside the selection bounds are transparent on the fill layer.
