3 · Handle mode comparison — Scale / Perspective / Shear
Scale (default)
White square handles at all 8 positions. Rotation circle above top-centre. Pivot cross-hair at centre.
Perspective
Orange diamond handles at corners only. Edge mid-point handles hidden. Box shows a perspective warp.
Shear
Green elongated handles at edge mid-points only. Corner handles hidden. Box shows a shear warp.
